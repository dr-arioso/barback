```
liqueurs:
  fruit:
    orange:
      triple_sec:

  herbal:
    amaro:
      fernet:
      alpine:

  nut:
    amaretto:
    nocino:

  cream:
    irish_cream:

```

Notes:
- “Amaro” is treated as a *subcategory of herbal liqueurs*, matching the US classification system.
- Functional roles (aperitif, digestif) are handled via StashTags.
